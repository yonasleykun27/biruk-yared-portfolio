# Biruk Yared | Architecture & Graphic Design Portfolio

An elegant, high-performance, interactive digital portfolio showcasing the architectural and graphic design works of **Biruk Yared**, a fifth-year architecture student and professional graphic designer based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia.

🌐 **Live Website:** [https://biruk-yared-portfolio.vercel.app/](https://biruk-yared-portfolio.vercel.app/)

---

## 🏛️ About the Designer
Biruk is a passionate fifth-year architecture student dedicated to human-centered, problem-solving spatial design. Inspired by urban life, public interaction, and the relationship between people and space, his work focuses on meaningful circulation and form development. 

Alongside architecture, Biruk has five years of professional experience in graphic design, collaborating with major companies like Weyera and creating custom apparel graphics for large-scale public and religious events.

## 📁 Featured Projects

### 1. **Apartment Design — *"Friendly Surveillance"***
* Reimagines the traditional, community-focused *kebele* spirit of 1960s Addis Ababa in a modern apartment building. Fosters social interaction through shared communal gardens and floor-level spaces.

### 2. **High School Design — *"Breaking The Paradox"***
* *Collaborators: Bisrateab Akadu, Biruk Yared, Sitra Ahmed*
* Transforms rigid classrooms into comfortable, home-like collaborative hubs. Features elevated reading and social spaces to emotionally connect students to their learning environment.

### 3. **Convention Center — *"Pathways of Possibility"***
* Inspired by the bustling, organic pathways of *Merkato*. The architecture uses multiple routes to invite curiosity and discovery rather than dictating a fixed direction.

### 4. **Luxury Business Hotel — *"World of Luxury"***
* An upscale commercial hospitality scheme showcasing structural systems, detailed landscape planning, and complex spatial coordination.

### 5. **High Rise Office Tower — *"The Civic Hierarchy"***
* A government municipality tower embodying the "Civic Hierarchy" of leadership. Elevates administrative and leadership wings to symbolize transparency and future-oriented responsibility.

## ⚡ Key Website Features

* **Premium Dark Aesthetic:** Curated color palette (charcoal tones with warm terracotta accents), sleek background grids, and glowing glassmorphic elements.
* **Interactive Blueprints (Lightbox v2):** 
  * Custom-built lightbox allows recruiters to view massive floor plans and presentation sheets.
  * Features **Mouse Wheel Zooming (up to 6x)**, **Click & Drag Panning** (map-style navigation), and **Double-Click toggles**.
* **35x Faster Performance:** 
  * All massive high-res sheets optimized and compressed (reducing total page size by **97.1%** from 110MB to 3.1MB).
  * Implemented browser-level **lazy loading** to ensure near-instant initial page loads.
* **Cloud-Streamed Documentation:** Large multi-page PDF blueprints are offloaded to high-speed cloud storage to provide instant, seamless document viewing.
